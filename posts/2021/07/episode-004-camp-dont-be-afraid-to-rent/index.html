Show Notes

After a long hiatus, WE ARE BACK! Camp season was busy and we talk about all of the adventures that we embarked on this summer. Join us as we talk about gear and approaches to production. We also talk about that sometimes renting is not a bad idea. Don’t be afraid to rent.
